About Us

Welcome to the official page of Matoshri College of Engineering - Esports Club. We are a passionate group of gamers, organizers, and enthusiasts dedicated to fostering a competitive gaming environment at our college.

Our club offers a variety of tournaments, skill development workshops, and a vibrant community where students can come together to pursue their love for gaming. We also organize events, both online and offline, for everyone to enjoy.
